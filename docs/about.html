    • About Me

    I have eight years of experience in data management roles across all aspects of the Data Life Cycle - Collection, Transformation, Analysis & Visualisation, Storage and Publishing in a range of industries polar science, marine science, education, civil engineering and most recently health care industry. I am a BSc (Hons) graduate in Environmental Science and spent the earlier part of my career in academia working in junior positions as Data Assistant and Data Manager, Polar Data Centre and Fieldwork Assistant, University of Aberdeen, then moved to more senior roles as a Data Scientist, British Oceanographic Data Centre and a Data Analyst, WSP.

    I am passionate about all things data, embracing different perspectives, collaboration, business change and mentoring and inspiring fellow data professionals. I strongly uphold my core values in openness, collaboration, integrity in everyday life to lead by example.

    I am an active MBCS member of the British Computing Society (BCS) and professionally listed on the Registration of IT Technicians (RiTTech).
