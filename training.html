    • Get Involved

    Get involved with dodgeball and come and train with us, in a fun and relaxed atmosphere with a great group of people. These sessions involve a mixture of drills to develop skill and general fitness as well as fun matches to put what's learnt into practice.

    All abilities welcome. Don’t worry if are a beginner, we will run through the rules, show you the basics and get you into games.

    We also play competitive matches and participate in open tournaments held by the UKDBA, throughout the year, in some cases training will be tailored to tournament practice

    Training held every Sunday from 19:30pm to 20:30pm in the Sports Hall, Colchester Leisure World.

    First sessions are free, thereafter £3 per session or £10 per month.

    Drop-in to a session or contact us for more information
